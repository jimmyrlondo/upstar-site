About Upstar Mental Health Services

Upstar focuses on providing outpatient and community mental healthcare to underserved individuals and children across Ohio.

Our Mission

Deliver accessible, high-quality behavioral healthcare that supports emotional wellness and healthier communities.

Our Approach

We build personalized care plans, collaborate with families and support networks, and meet clients where they are.

Who We Serve

Children, teens, adults, and families seeking therapy, counseling, case management, and school-based support.
