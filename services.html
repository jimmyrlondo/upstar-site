Services

We provide outpatient and community behavioral health services for children, teens, adults, and families.

Individual, Family, and Group Therapy

Our therapists work with each client to understand their needs and set goals that reduce mental health symptoms and improve daily functioning. Services may include one-on-one therapy, family sessions, or group counseling.

Chemical Dependency Counseling

We support clients facing substance use challenges through counseling and structured care planning aligned to each person’s strengths and recovery goals.

Case Management

Our behavioral health specialists provide advocacy and support in community settings to help clients access resources and gain independence.

  • Client and physician referrals
  • Clothing vouchers and housing application support
  • Disability, Medicaid, and Social Security applications
  • Driver’s license obtaining/reinstatement support
  • Education and career planning/job skill development
  • Coping skills for anger and depression

School-Based Services and Prevention Groups

Students can receive support through individual counseling, group counseling, and at-home services when needed. Prevention groups focus on decision-making, anger management, violence prevention, and social skills development.

Typical diagnoses include ADHD, mood disorders, behavior disorders, anxiety, adjustment reactions, and post-traumatic stress.
